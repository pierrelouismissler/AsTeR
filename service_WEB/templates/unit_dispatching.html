Unit Dispatching

AsTeR is made to help the firefighters and the emergency forces on the ground. By leading them with the most efficient way, the rescues can be processed easily and even more securely than previously.

During the IBM Call for Code hackathon at UC Berkeley, we decided to build a dispatcher working in real-time including a lot of different information from the ground and from external platforms as weather

The dispatching at AsTeR is build with Djikstra algorithms providing the faster trajectories from the current location of the unit to the destination. The trajectories can be update in real time if the unit want it or of new information appear in our database.

The units are sharing their location in real time, which will give us a way to manage the different units on a specific area. After natural disasters, the efficiency of emergency services is possible if everyone have the right information. AsTeR provide a convenient solution to manage a lot of units in the time during crisis.
