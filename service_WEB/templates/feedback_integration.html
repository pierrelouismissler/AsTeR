Feedback Integration

The AsTeR experience provides a tool to help the units on the ground to inform about the situation. Throught an app, they have just to spend few seconds to send the relevant information.

The app has been created from the perspective of a firefighter. The use of the radio can be reinforced by our aggregator of information. We listed several situations which have to be communicate as quickly as possible:

Blocked routes:

When a truck can't go further or see a blocked route in the area, the information is immediately aggregate to change the trajectories of next trucks. We are focusing mainly on the natural disaster which could create some damages on the routes used for emergency.

Danger in the area:

When the unit using the app is victim or see a danger in the area. The current location will be immediately saved in our database and will affect all the trajectories for other units.

New victims:

When the unit find a new victim during the operation. They could click the button and if they have more time, the unit can save the personal information of the victim to be faster with the coordination between emergency services.

Image classification:

Pictures taken on the field by the rescue team. By passing them through image classification, encountered problems are automatically detected. It allows us to update the dispatching map (graph) in real-time, which is exploited via a Dijkstra algorithm for optimal trajectories determination.
